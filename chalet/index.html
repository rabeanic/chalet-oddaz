The Chalet

360 m² of contemporary alpine comfort, completed in 2026.

Chalet Oddaz has been designed for refined mountain living: generous shared spaces, quiet bedroom suites, wellness facilities and technology that makes stays comfortable year-round.

Ground floor

  • Wellness area with balneotherapy bath, sauna, music and sofa.
  • Luxury double bedroom with en-suite shower room.
  • Dormitory for six adults, with individual reading lights and power points.
  • Dormitory en-suite bathroom with bath and shower.

Living level

  • 100 m² open living space with private large TV room.
  • Central kitchen and Brazilian granite island with bar stools.
  • 3.6 m dining table seating 14 guests comfortably.
  • 8 ft pool table, gas fireplace and large corner sofa.
  • Access to west and east terraces.

Second floor

  • Master bedroom with bathroom, double shower and balcony.
  • Two further luxury en-suite double bedrooms.
  • Each bedroom has a small workspace connected to the internet.

Garden and exterior

  • 900 m² landscaped garden.
  • Private pétanque area.
  • Carport and parking for at least four additional cars.
  • Large gas-connected BBQ on the eastern terrace, designed for 24 people.
