Chalet

Three hundred and ten square metres of alpine living, completed in 2026.

Chalet Oddaz has been conceived as a place of quiet exception — generous shared spaces for family and friends, intimate bedroom suites, a wellness retreat and the discreet comfort of a home that lives beautifully through every season.

Ground floor

  • A wellness area with balneotherapy bath, sauna, music and a sofa for unhurried afternoons.
  • A luxury double bedroom with en-suite shower room.
  • A dormitory for six adults, each with individual reading light and power point.
  • En-suite bathroom with bath and shower.

Living level

  • One hundred square metres of open living, with a private television room.
  • A central kitchen and Brazilian granite island with bar stools.
  • A 3.6 m dining table — fourteen guests seated in comfort.
  • An 8 ft pool table, gas fireplace and generous corner sofa.
  • Access to east and west terraces.

Second floor

  • A master suite with bathroom, double shower and private balcony.
  • Two further luxury en-suite double bedrooms.
  • Each bedroom with a small workspace and internet connection.

Garden and exterior

  • A 900 m² landscaped garden.
  • A private pétanque court.
  • Carport and parking for at least four additional cars.
  • A large gas-connected barbecue on the eastern terrace, designed for twenty-four.
