Activities

Samoëns: authentic Haute-Savoie, open valley views and Grand Massif access.

The chalet is a five-minute walk from the centre of Samoëns with its bars, restaurants, markets and galleries. Geneva Airport is around one hour away; Cluses SNCF station is around 25 minutes away.

Winter

Skiing, snowshoeing, ice-skating and paragliding, with easy access to Samoëns 1600 via the Grand Massif Express.

Official winter activities

Summer

Cycling, white-water sports, canyoning, tennis, padel, hiking, climbing, via ferrata, paragliding, golf and lake swimming.

Official summer activities

Views

Mont Criou, the Mont Blanc massif, the Dents Blanches and the Cirque du Fer-à-Cheval cliffs shape the chalet’s panorama.
