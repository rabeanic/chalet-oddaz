Activities

Samoëns and the Grand Massif — a valley of patrimony and possibility.

Five minutes on foot from the centre of Samoëns — its cafés, restaurants, markets and galleries. Geneva Airport is around one hour away; Cluses station, twenty-five minutes.

Winter

Skiing, snowshoeing, ice-skating and paragliding — with Samoëns 1600 reached easily via the Grand Massif Express.

Official winter activities

Summer

Cycling, white-water sports, canyoning, tennis, padel, hiking, climbing, via ferrata, paragliding, golf and lake swimming — the mountains in their fullest expression.

Official summer activities

Panorama

Mont Criou, the Mont Blanc massif, the Dents Blanches and the Cirque du Fer-à-Cheval — the horizon that frames every day at Chalet Oddaz.
