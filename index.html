The setting

Authentic village life, wide valley light and exceptional mountain panoramas.

Set a five-minute walk from the centre of Samoëns, Chalet Oddaz enjoys views towards Mont Criou, the Mont Blanc massif, the Dents Blanches and the cliffs of the Cirque du Fer-à-Cheval.

The chalet sits close to the famous Tour de France road to Joux Plane, making it a natural base for cycling enthusiasts in summer and cross-country skiers in winter.
