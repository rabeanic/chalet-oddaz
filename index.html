Chalet Oddaz

★★★★★

A refined alpine retreat in one of Haute-Savoie’s most authentic villages.

Chalet Oddaz is a south-facing 310 m² luxury chalet for up to 14 guests, combining generous entertaining spaces, refined interiors, wellness facilities and open mountain views.

The setting

A five-minute walk from the heart of Samoëns, Chalet Oddaz combines village life with expansive mountain views, abundant year-round sunlight and immediate access to one of the French Alps’ most diverse four-season playgrounds.
