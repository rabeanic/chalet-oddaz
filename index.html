A refined and welcoming retreat above the heart of Samoëns

Facing south above Samoëns, this 310 m² chalet welcomes fourteen guests into generous volumes, quietly refined interiors and open mountain light — a place made for long tables, fireside evenings and unhurried mornings among family and friends.

The setting

Five minutes on foot from the heart of Samoëns, Chalet Oddaz holds a singular position between village and mountain — the open valley before you, the changing light of the seasons, and the quiet authenticity of one of Haute-Savoie’s most cherished communes.
